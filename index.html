About Us

Bravado Security & Surveillance was founded by the late Lt. Col. H.C. Sharma, a decorated veteran whose values of discipline, courage, and integrity continue to guide our mission. Today, the firm is led by his son Sanjeev Sharma, an accomplished security strategist with over 20 years of industry leadership, supported by Subedar (Retd.) Kartar Singh. Under their guidance, Bravado provides compliant, vigilant, and scalable security solutions to industries, corporations, and critical infrastructure across Delhi NCR.
