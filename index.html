Our Legacy & Mission

Bravado Security & Solutions was founded by the late Lt. Col. H.C. Sharma, a decorated Army veteran with an unwavering commitment to discipline, leadership, and national service.

Following a distinguished career in the Indian Army, he established Bravado Security with the vision of delivering security services built on ethics, vigilance, and military-grade professionalism.

His values remain the cornerstone of everything we do — a legacy that continues to inspire our operations to this day.
