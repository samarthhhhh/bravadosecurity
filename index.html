Our Legacy & Mission

Bravado Security & Solutions was founded by the late Lt. Col. H.C. Sharma, a decorated Army veteran known for his unwavering discipline, leadership, and service to the nation.

Today, his legacy continues under the leadership of his son Sanjeev Sharma, a security strategist with 20+ years of experience, alongside Subedar (Retd.) Kartar Singh.

Together, they lead a team committed to delivering ethical, vigilant, and compliant security services across Delhi NCR for corporates, industries, and high-security zones.
